Leadership Profile

A Visionary
Leader

Lion Engr. Faheem Iqbal Ch. is a distinguished leader with a strong academic foundation in engineering and law. As Executive Director of Iqbal Group of Companies, he has led with unwavering commitment to service, innovation, and community empowerment.

A PEC Registered Engineer and Bar Council Registered Advocate, he combines technical expertise with legal acumen — a rare combination that drives his visionary leadership in both the corporate and humanitarian spheres.

Dedicated to building a shared future through inclusion, compassion, and transformative service, he now seeks to lead Lions Clubs International District 305-N2 as District Governor for 2026–27.
